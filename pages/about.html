About Our University

Lunaris University is a prestigious institution dedicated to academic excellence and innovation. Founded in 1990, we have been at the forefront of higher education, providing students with world-class learning experiences.

Our campus spans over 200 acres of beautiful landscape, featuring state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant community of scholars, researchers, and students from around the world.
